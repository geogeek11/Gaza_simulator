About

The goal of this visualization is to view the palestinian disaster from its own perspective. Also to know more about Palestinian diaspora. And the human impact of the israeli-arab conflict.

The number of victims and refugees are based on recent census and predictions. The ratios used are as below:
- population inside Gaza presents 14% of the total population. 44% among them are refugees.
- population inside Israel presents 35% of the total population of palestinians.
- 73% of population are refugees.
- 48% of population are refugees in neighbour countries.
- 3% of population are refugees in other countries.
- 0.036% of population are victims.
- Gaza the area out of control of israeli occupation presents 1% of the area of Palestine ( before 1948 ).
